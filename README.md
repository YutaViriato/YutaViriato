## Profile

I am a **Senior Full-Stack Engineer**. I design and ship end-to-end web systems: data modeling, APIs, authentication, interfaces, and production infrastructure.

I own architecture, close trade-offs, raise code quality, and leave products stable enough to grow. I do not just drop features. I take responsibility for the system.

I work TypeScript-first, with clear contracts between UI, API, and data. Multi-tenant design, permissions, RLS, and predictable deploys are defaults, not later phases.

---

## What I deliver

**Systems and product**
- Full-stack architecture with clear boundaries
- Multi-tenant products with authentication and data isolation
- APIs and domains built to evolve without collapsing
- Clean, fast interfaces with product intent

**Engineering**
- End-to-end TypeScript with serious typing and contracts
- Well-modeled PostgreSQL (constraints, indexes, RLS when it matters)
- Enough observability to operate production without flying blind
- Predictable CI/CD and deploys with Vercel, Docker, and clean environments

**Seniority**
- Technical decisions with cost, risk, and maintenance in view
- Code review with standards
- Refactors that pay down debt without blocking the roadmap
- Ability to lead the technical execution of a product
